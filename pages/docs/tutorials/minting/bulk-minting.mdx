# Bulk Minting 

## Overview

This guide outlines the steps to setup your media files and metadata and then mint as NFTs on Loopring. This process can be particularly useful when you have many NFTs that you need to mint at once and want free storage.

<Callout type="warning" emoji="ðŸ“£">
  Completing the airdrop will involve fees from both Loopring and Maize.
</Callout>

## Requirements ðŸ“‹

- Maize Software: Make sure it's [downloaded](../download-maize) and [setup](../setup-maize) correctly.
- Metadatas and NFTs folder (See 1/1 Generation[1/1 Generation](../one-of-one-creation))
- Optional Animations folder

## Steps

<Steps>

    ### Initiate `Bulk Minting` 

    Navigate to the Minting Section within the Maize Menu. Here, you'll find the 'Bulk Minting' option. Click on it to start.
    
  ### Input General Information

Select the collection you want to mint to, Loopring Fee (in ETH or LRC), Maize Fee (in ETH, LRC, or PEPE), and the path to your Metadatas and NFTs folder (optional Animations folder).

  ![Ipfs Step One](/images/tutorials/mintingToIpfs/IpfsStepOne.png)
  <ImageStacking image1="/images/tutorials/mintingToIpfs/IpfsStepTwo.png" image2="/images/tutorials/mintingToIpfs/IpfsStepThree.png" />

  - Copy the generated CID and paste it into Maize.

  #### Select Your Fees
  - For your Loopring Fee you can select between ETH or LRC.
  - For your Maize Fee you can select from ETH, LRC, and PEPE
  <Callout type="warning" emoji="ðŸ“£">
  The Maize Fee is 0.001 USD per transaction converted to the selected currency.
  </Callout>

  #### Metadatas and NFTs Folder path
- Click Browse and select your Metadatas and NFTs folder. Verify the authenticity of your metadata. 
  ![Select Root Directory](/images/tutorials/mintingToIpfs/SelectRootDirectory.png)
<Callout type="info" emoji="ðŸ“˜">
Incorrect or corrupt metadata can result in unusable or misleading NFTs.
</Callout>
- Click Preview to review your entries.

  ![Step One Done](/images/tutorials/mintingToIpfs/StepOneDone.png)

### Input Metadata Information
Repeat the steps from the 'Input General Information' section but for the Metadatas Folder CID.

#### Select NFT Amount
- Choose the number of NFTs to mint per type (between 1 and 100,000).  
- Click Mint.

![Step Two Done](/images/tutorials/mintingToIpfs/StepTwoDone.png)

### Review the Results
- The application will auto-open an audit file. Confirm that everything is accurate and review the data on Loopring.io.

![Audit File](/images/tutorials/mintingToIpfs/Audit.png)
  ### Congratulations! ðŸŽ‰
You have successfully minted NFTs on Loopring!
</Steps>
