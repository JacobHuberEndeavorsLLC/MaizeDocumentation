## Welcome to Maize!

Maize is an open-sourced, community driven NFT Toolkit on Loopring. I'm working on developing a simple application for creators and collectors to use to manage their NFTs on [Loopring](https://loopring.org/#/about).

## Download and How-To

For detailed information on downloading, setting up, and utilizing the application, navigate to the left panel where you'll find step-by-step guides and tutorials.

## Contribute to Maize

Maize is an open-source, community-driven project that welcomes any contribution. This includes both technical and non-technical contributions.

Take a look at the [contribution guide](https://github.com/cobmin/Maize/blob/main/ContributionGuide.md.md) to get started as a contributor!
